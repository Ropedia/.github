# Ropedia 👋

**From Xperience to Physical & Spatial Intelligence.**

Ropedia builds data, tools, and infrastructure to **capture, structure, and scale real-world human Xperience** for physical AI 🤖🌍

We believe the next generation of embodied intelligence will not come only from bigger models, but from better **grounded experience** — how humans see, move, act, and interact in the real world.

## 🚀 What we’re building

At Ropedia, we are building the foundation layer for physical AI:

- 📷 **Capture systems** for recording human Xperience in the real world
- 🧩 **Multimodal data pipelines** for turning raw recordings into structured signals
- 🧠 **Learning-ready datasets** for world models, robotics, and embodied agents
- 🌐 **Infrastructure** for scaling physical and spatial intelligence data

Our mission is simple:

> **Intelligence begins with Xperience.**

## 📦 New release: Xperience-10M

We just released **Xperience-10M**, our largest human Xperience dataset to date 🎉

Xperience-10M is a large-scale **4D multimodal dataset** spanning:

- 🎥 video
- 🔊 audio
- 🗺️ depth
- 📍 pose
- 🕺 motion capture
- 📈 inertial sensing
- 📝 language annotation

It is designed for teams building:

- 🧠 world models
- 🤖 robotics systems
- 🚶 embodied agents

Rather than isolated frames or synthetic trajectories, Xperience-10M preserves **temporally consistent, embodied human experience in real environments**.

## ✨ Why it matters

Physical AI needs data that is:

- grounded in the real world
- synchronized across modalities
- rich in motion, perception, and context
- usable for both pretraining and downstream tasks

Xperience-10M is built to help models learn **world-grounded behavior** from human experience at scale.
